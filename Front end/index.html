Basic Concept of the project

Our project sounds too good to be true and in a sense it is. The accuracy of our project is not that high and has to depend on the soil information on site.

We had a basic idea to predict landslide depending on three major aspects, the change in altitude at a place, its climate and soil nature. We can also take in account of the past landslides and its frequency.

All the informations are taken from Google Earth Engine which was given access for study purpose only. The basic application used are discussed

  • Google Eath for finding the slope and climate information and JavaScript for programming.
  • Python for calculating our conclusion based on three major information from Google Eath.
  • GitHub is used for version control. Further upon receiving predictions about any landslides Twilio can be used to send messaged to nearby people.

We used basic rainfall, slope and soil data from Google Earth Engine to determine where a landslide is likely to occur.
